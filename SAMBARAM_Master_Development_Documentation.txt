SAMBARAM MASTER DEVELOPMENT ROADMAP (MVP)

================================================== 1. PRODUCT VISION
==================================================

SAMBARAM is a premium event lead marketplace.

Our responsibility: - Generate premium event enquiries. - Verify every
serious enquiry. - Connect customers with trusted vendors.

Our responsibility ends after introducing a verified customer to the
selected vendor. We are NOT an event management company.

Core principle: TRUST BEFORE SCALE.

================================================== 2. CUSTOMER FLOW
==================================================

Customer sees Meta Ad / Instagram Reel ↓ Visits website ↓ Uses AI Event
Planner ↓ Submits enquiry ↓ Lead stored in database ↓ CRM receives
notification ↓ Team calls customer ↓ Verify: - Event date - Budget -
Location - Requirements - Timeline - Whether enquiry is genuine ↓ Update
notes and corrected budget ↓ Mark VERIFIED ↓ Publish to Vendor
Marketplace

Optional: - Ask for Instagram / LinkedIn only if customer is
comfortable. - Never make social proof mandatory. - Phone conversation
remains primary verification.

================================================== 3. CRM
==================================================

Lead List Lead Details Internal Notes Customer Information AI Summary
Planning Preferences Notification Status

Statuses: New Contacted Verified Published Closed

Future: Dashboard Search Filters Vendor assignment history

================================================== 4. VENDOR PLATFORM
==================================================

Vendor Types:

1.  Full Event Managers
2.  Photographers

Store: Business Portfolio Cities Experience Past Events Contact
Availability

Vendor Levels:

Bronze Silver Gold Elite

Only trusted vendors should receive premium events.

================================================== 5. VENDOR
VERIFICATION ==================================================

Collect: - Identity/business documents where appropriate - Portfolio -
Social presence - References if available - Manual interview

After onboarding: Approve or Reject.

================================================== 6. VERIFIED LEAD
MARKETPLACE ==================================================

Only VERIFIED enquiries appear.

Vendor initially sees:

-   Budget
-   Date
-   City
-   Event Type
-   AI Summary
-   Internal verification notes

Customer phone/email hidden.

Vendor clicks: Acquire Lead

First acquired lead for every new vendor: FREE

This helps build trust.

================================================== 7. SECURITY
==================================================

Implement: - Rate limiting - CAPTCHA when suspicious - OTP
verification - Spam protection - Abuse monitoring

Do not permanently block normal users sharing one IP.

================================================== 8. NOTIFICATIONS
==================================================

Customer: Submission Updates

Vendor: New lead Lead acquired

Internal: Telegram CRM alerts

================================================== 9. LAUNCH PLAN
==================================================

Finish platform Deploy Onboard vendors Run Meta ads Verify leads Publish
verified leads Collect first vendor feedback Then finalize pricing.

================================================== 10. BUSINESS IDEAS
DISCUSSED ==================================================

-   Human verification before publishing.
-   Vendor trust is more important than customer trust.
-   First acquired lead free.
-   Vendor capability levels.
-   Notes visible to vendors.
-   Marketplace should focus on premium events.
-   Photographers may become a separate low-touch marketplace in the
    future.
-   Pricing should be validated with real vendors before locking it.
